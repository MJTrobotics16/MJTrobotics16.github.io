About Me

I am an aspiring Electrical Engineer with interests in robotics, autonomous systems, CAD design, programming, and space exploration technology.

Ever since I was young, I have been fascinated by the process of building and creating various structures and prototypes of concepts that interested me. What began with constructing complex LEGO creations gradually evolved into a deeper interest in designing robotic systems, electrical circuits, and mechanical mechanisms. As my curiosity grew, so did my relentless urge to understand how hardware and software work together to solve real-world problems.

As I began attending engineering camps and enrolling in engineering and technology courses, I became even more intrigued by this vast field and the countless innovations, discoveries, and challenges that continue to shape our world. These experiences opened my eyes to how complex and interconnected modern technology truly is, and they strengthened my desire to learn how innovative systems are designed, tested, and improved.

Today, I am particularly interested in the intersection of electrical engineering, robotics, and autonomous technology. Through engineering projects, programming, and technical design, I hope to continue developing the skills necessary to contribute to future innovations in robotics and advanced space systems.

My dream is to pursue a career designing, building, and programming autonomous robotic platforms, including rover systems and other technologies used in exploration, research, and discovery.
