About Me

I am an aspiring Electrical Engineer with interests in robotics, autonomous systems, CAD design, programming, and space exploration technology.

My dream is to pursue a career designing, building, and programming autonomous robotic platforms, including rover systems and other technologies used in exploration, research, and discovery.
