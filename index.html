About Me

I am an aspiring Electrical Engineer with interests in robotics, autonomous systems, CAD design, programming, and space exploration technology.

Ever since I was young, I have been fascinated by the process of building and creating various structures and prototypes of concepts that interested me. What began with constructing complex LEGO creations gradually evolved into a deeper interest in designing robotic systems, electrical circuits, and mechanical mechanisms.

As I began attending engineering camps and enrolling in engineering and technology courses, I became even more intrigued by this vast field and the countless innovations, discoveries, and challenges that continue to shape our world.

My dream is to pursue a career designing, building, and programming autonomous robotic platforms, including rover systems and other technologies used in exploration, research, and discovery.
